# Brand Content Gate

Validates the organization content against brand voice standards, kill list, CTA alignment, and humanizer requirements before any content is approved for publication or send.

---

## Brand Voice (Wise Alchemist × Seth Godin) (25% weight)

- Tone: Confident, grounded, educational — never salesy or hype-driven
- Perspective: Guide who has walked the path, not guru or authority figure
- Language: Accessible science, not academic jargon
- Emotional register: Calm conviction, not urgency or fear
- Seth Godin filter: Would Seth publish this? Is it generous? Does it create tension that demands resolution?

**Scoring**: Score 0–100 on how well the content embodies this voice. Deduct points for urgency language, authority posturing, jargon, or hype.

---

## Primary Kill List (30% weight, BLOCKING)

These words and phrases MUST NOT appear in any the organization content:

- "Sacred" (when describing ceremonies or experiences)
- "Miracle" / "Miraculous"
- "Therapy" / "Therapeutic" (legal liability — we are not licensed therapists)
- "Spirit Guide" / "Spiritual Guide"
- "Breakthrough" (when describing psychedelic experiences)
- "Cure" / "Heal" / "Treatment" (medical claims)
- "Shaman" / "Shamanic" (cultural appropriation concern)
- "Journey" as a noun for the psychedelic experience (overused)
- "Medicine" when referring to psilocybin (regulatory concern)

**Scoring**: Any primary kill list word = automatic -30 points. Two or more = hard fail regardless of other scores.

---

## Extended Kill List (15% weight, WARNING)

Flag but do not hard-fail. Each occurrence reduces score:

- "Life-changing" (cliché)
- "Transform your life" (over-promising)
- "Ancient wisdom" (appropriative undertone)
- "Unlock your potential" (self-help cliché)
- "Spiritual awakening" (vague, unprovable)
- Excessive use of "we" without specificity
- Any medical or health claims without citation

**Scoring**: -5 per occurrence, max -15 total deduction from this section.

---

## CTA Alignment (15% weight)

- Primary CTA is clear and visible
- CTA language matches brand voice (not "BUY NOW", "ACT FAST")
- CTA leads to correct destination URL
- CTA promise matches landing page content (no bait-and-switch)
- Maximum 2 distinct CTAs per email (focus)

**Scoring**: Score 0–100. Deduct for each violation. Missing CTA in an email = 0 for this section.

---

## Teaching Insight (10% weight)

- Content provides genuine educational value
- At least one data point, study citation, or expert perspective
- Reader learns something they did not know before
- Content would be worth reading even without the CTA

**Scoring**: Score 0–100. Pure promotional content with no teaching value scores 0.

---

## Humanizer Check (5% weight)

- No robotic or templated-sounding phrases
- Varied sentence length (not all the same structure)
- Natural transitions between paragraphs
- Sounds like it was written by a person, not generated by AI
- References the operator's or the organization's specific experience where appropriate

**Scoring**: Score 0–100. Mechanical phrasing, repetitive structure, or generic filler language reduces score.

---

## Scoring Summary

| Section | Weight | Hard Fail Condition |
|---|---|---|
| Brand Voice | 25% | None (score deductions only) |
| Primary Kill List | 30% | Any kill list word = -30; two or more = hard fail |
| Extended Kill List | 15% | None (score deductions, max -15) |
| CTA Alignment | 15% | None (score deductions only) |
| Teaching Insight | 10% | None (score deductions only) |
| Humanizer Check | 5% | None (score deductions only) |

**Weighted sum = overall brand content score.**

- Hard fail: any primary kill list violation of two or more words, regardless of total score
- Pass threshold: overall score ≥ 85
- Below threshold: route to Quill for revision with specific failure notes
