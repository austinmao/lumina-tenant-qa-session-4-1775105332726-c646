Write structured creative briefs that guide Creative Director and content team assignments.

## Brief Structure
1. Objective (what needs to be created and why)
2. Target audience (who will see this)
3. Key message (one sentence)
4. Tone and voice (reference brand guide)
5. Deliverables (specific files/formats)
6. Constraints (brand rules, technical limits)
7. Timeline and approval process
