# Scoring Rubric — Trend Intelligence

## Dimensions (weighted)

| Dimension | Weight | Measures |
|---|---|---|
| Momentum | 30% | Coverage velocity this week vs prior 30 days. Is this accelerating? |
| Breadth | 25% | Cross-domain presence: news + community + research = max score. Single-source = low. |
| Significance | 25% | Real-world consequentiality: policy change > opinion piece > social media chatter |
| Brand relevance | 20% | Direct impact on retreat demand, participant trust, public perception, or brand positioning |

## Signal Labels

- **High signal** — Multiple credible sources, real-world consequence, durable importance
- **Mixed** — Some credible coverage but unclear trajectory or conflicting narratives
- **Hype-driven** — Loud but shallow; driven by social amplification, not substance
- **Polarizing** — Strong signal but contested; requires careful framing

## Brand Relevance Scoring

Score HIGHER when the trend affects:
- Retreat demand or participant decision-making
- Public trust in psychedelic-assisted experiences
- Safety, ethics, or practitioner standards (the organization's authority space)
- Legal access in the organization's operating regions
- Integration practices (the organization's post-retreat differentiator)

Score LOWER when the trend:
- Is purely business/financial (stock moves, M&A) with no participant impact
- Involves substances the organization doesn't work with and no regulatory spillover
- Is geographic to a region the organization doesn't operate in with no precedent value

## Brand Positioning Guardrails (mandatory flags)

| Topic type | Flag | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Therapeutic/clinical claims | "Caution: framing risk" | Do not present as endorsement. Use "research suggests" language. |
| Peyote / indigenous medicine | "Caution: stewardship sensitivity" | Consult Austin before using in any content. Never appropriate without reciprocity framing. |
| Safety incidents / adverse events | "Caution: sensitive" | Lead with care for affected individuals. Frame as learning, not distancing. |
| Legal gray areas | "Caution: legal framing" | State facts only. Do not advise on legality. |
| Competitor controversies | "Caution: positioning" | Never pile on. If relevant, frame as industry-wide learning. |

## Ranking Guidance

A trend ranks near the top if it is:
- Broadly discussed AND rapidly rising (momentum + breadth)
- Highly consequential even with lower chatter (significance override)
- Especially relevant to the organization's audience and positioning (relevance override)

Explain non-obvious rankings briefly in the briefing output.
