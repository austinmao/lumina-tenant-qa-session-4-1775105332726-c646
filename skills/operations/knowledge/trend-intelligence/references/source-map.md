# Source Map — Trend Intelligence

## Tier 1 — Always search (every run)

### News & Industry

Search clusters (use web search tool):
- "psilocybin news this week"
- "psychedelic therapy regulation [current month] [current year]"
- "ayahuasca retreat news"
- "ibogaine legislation"
- "ketamine clinic policy"
- "MDMA therapy FDA"
- "microdosing research"

Priority outlets: Psychedelic Alpha, STAT News, Nature, The Guardian health, MAPS.org, Chacruna Institute

### Community Discourse

- Reddit: r/PsychedelicTherapy, r/microdosing, r/Psychonaut, r/shroomstocks
- Look for: repeated themes across threads, unusual engagement velocity, recurring concerns/hopes/controversies, practitioner pain points
- Treat as signal source, not proof

### Policy & Regulation

- MAPS.org press releases and news
- State legislature trackers (search: "psychedelic bill [state]" for active states: Oregon, Colorado, Minnesota, California, Massachusetts, Connecticut)
- FDA press releases (search: "FDA psychedelic" or "FDA psilocybin")
- DEA scheduling updates

## Tier 2 — Search when relevant

### Clinical Research

- PubMed: search "psilocybin OR ayahuasca OR ibogaine OR MDMA therapy" filtered to last 7-14 days
- ClinicalTrials.gov: new trial registrations
- Major conference announcements (APA, ACNP, MAPS)

### Business & Industry

- Company news: Compass Pathways, MindMed, Atai Life Sciences, Cybin, Numinus
- Crunchbase/press releases for new funding rounds
- Clinic network expansions or closures

### Culture & Discourse

- Integration and ethics discourse
- Indigenous reciprocity conversations
- Safety incidents or media controversies
- Celebrity/influencer mentions (signal for public perception shifts)

## Search Discipline

- Cover at least 3 different source types per run
- Cover both top-down reporting AND bottom-up community discourse
- When one story dominates, still search for what's emerging underneath it
- Cite load-bearing claims with source URLs
