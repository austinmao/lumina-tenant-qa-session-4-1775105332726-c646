# SEO & GEO Content Writing

Write comprehensive, engaging content optimized for both traditional search engines and AI citation. Create content with natural keyword integration, E-E-A-T signal inclusion, and AI-extractable passage structure. Use when creating SEO-optimized articles, landing pages, or knowledge base content.

## When to Use

- Writing SEO-optimized blog posts or articles
- Creating content briefs with keyword targets
- Producing content with E-E-A-T signals for expertise
- Structuring content for AI citability (GEO optimization)

## Content Creation Framework

### Introduction (50-100 words)
- Hook the reader immediately with a compelling fact or question
- State the value proposition clearly
- Include primary keyword naturally in first sentence
- Set clear expectations for what the reader will learn

### Body Content
- Comprehensive topic coverage with logical flow
- Question-based H2/H3 headings matching real user queries
- Natural keyword placement with semantic variations
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences) for scannability
- Supporting data, examples, and expert citations
- Definition patterns for key terms ("X is [definition].")

### Conclusion
- Summarize key points concisely
- Clear call-to-action
- Reinforce value delivered

## Quality Standards

- **Keyword density:** 0.5-1.5% (natural integration, never forced)
- **Readability:** Grade 8-10 reading level (Flesch 60-70)
- **Paragraph length:** 2-3 sentences per paragraph
- **Headings:** Question-based where possible, keyword-enriched
- **Lists and tables:** Use for any comparison or sequential information

## E-E-A-T Elements

- First-hand experience mentions ("When we tested...", "In our experience...")
- Specific examples and case studies with measurable outcomes
- Data and statistics with named sources and dates
- Expert perspective with clear professional judgment
- Practical, actionable advice (not generic platitudes)

## GEO Citability Optimization

- **Optimal passage length:** 134-167 words per extractable block
- **Self-contained passages:** Each paragraph understandable without surrounding context
- **Answer-first structure:** Direct answer in first sentence, then supporting detail
- **Statistical density:** 3-5 specific numbers per 500 words with named sources
- **Definition patterns:** "X is [definition]" increases AI citation rate by 2.1x

## Output

Full article package including:
- Complete article at target word count
- 3-5 title variations with character counts
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Key takeaways / summary bullet points
- Internal linking suggestions
- FAQ section with 5-10 questions (if applicable)
